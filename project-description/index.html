How To Play


Space Fighters is a retro-style, multiplayer, top-down shooter game. The playerâ€™s goal is to navigate the far reaches of the cosmos without getting destroyed by the other players out in depths of space.

Upon visiting our website, users are met with a splash screen which prompts them to enter a name, shows them game instructions, and readies them for action. After clicking the start button, they are thrown into a lobby with the other players, and the battle begins!

Visually, a user will see their ship at the center of the screen. Each ship is equipped with a gun which can rotate 360 degrees around the ship. The players can move around using the WASD keys, aim using the mouse, and shoot by clicking. They gain points by killing other enemies in battle. Once they are hit with a bullet, they themselves die. They are then sent back to the original splash screen which tells them the score, and asks them to play again.
